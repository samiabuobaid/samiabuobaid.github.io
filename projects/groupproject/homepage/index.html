“J’Eat Yet? Did I what? J’Eat Yet? I have no idea what you are saying.” If you have had this conversation with a Pittsburgher, you’re not the only one so don’t worry. "J’Eat Yet" is a common slang word for the native people of Pittsburgh. If someone asks you “J’Eat Yet?” They're asking if you “have eaten yet?” If you’re like most people you just smile and nod at these Pittsburghers who have weird words for things (i.e. "buggy" for a shopping cart). Or, if you’re like the other, you just give that look of confusion and just keep asking what the heck they are saying until they answer in a language that you can actually understand.


If you like Google Maps to help show what’s around to eat and you like Yelp for the ratings, then you will like this even more. The goal for this site was to first show you all the locations of restaurants in Allegheny County. We want to show a couple different data visualizations that:

  1. Showed the location, price, and the name of the restaurant
  2. Which kinds of food were prevalent in each zip code
  3. How many restaurants where in each price range (using Yelp's scale)
  4. Which streets had the most restaurants

We hoped that these different break-downs would be able to help people pick where they wanted to go more easily, and maybe even keep that common argument of “where do you want to eat?” at a minimum.
